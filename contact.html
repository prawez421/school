Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Alibros School.

How can I apply for admission?

You can apply online through our admission form or visit the school office for assistance.

What classes does Alibros School offer?

We provide education from Nursery to higher classes with a balanced academic curriculum.

What are the school timings?

Our school operates from Monday to Saturday, generally from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.

What documents are required for admission?

Required documents include a birth certificate, Aadhaar card, previous school records, and photographs.
